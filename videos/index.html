Tutorial Videos

Below are all the tutorial videos created for EduCraft. They will introduce you to the key concepts required to understand how to play this mod.

Crafting sticks

Making sticks is a key skill to learn. In order to make operators, you first need sticks.

Operator bench

The operator bench is used to make your mathematical operators out of sticks.

Unlocking doors

Often, a door will be locked, and will need a specific item in order to unlock it.

Ordering bench

An ordering bench accepts three numbers, and will return a key if the numbers are in order from smallest to largest.

Note that some benches will only accept odd numbers, or even numbers"!

Calculator

The most important element of the mod! Use it to create new numbers from ones that you collect in the world.
